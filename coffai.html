Vortex Chat Rules

Please review and accept the Vortex Chat Rules to continue.

1. Respectful Behavior

Treat fellow members with respect and courtesy. Avoid any behavior that could be seen as disrespectful, offensive, or harmful.

Be mindful of your language and tone in interactions. While profanity is allowed, refrain from using derogatory or inflammatory language, especially hate speech or slurs.

2. Content Standards

Share content that is suitable for a diverse audience. Steer clear of posting material that is defamatory, abusive, obscene, threatening, NSFW, or otherwise inappropriate.

Do not share content that promotes violence, discrimination, hate speech, or illegal activities.

3. Respect for Privacy

Respect the privacy of others. Never share personal information about fellow members without their consent.

Do not engage in stalking, harassment, or any invasion of privacy.

4. Spam and Advertising

Avoid spamming Vortex with unnecessary messages or advertisements.

Advertising external products or services without permission is prohibited.

5. Language

English is the primary language for communication within the community. Using other languages may hinder moderation efforts and is therefore not allowed.

Use English for all communications, including DMs, feedback, and chat messages.

6. Reporting Violations

If you see any violations of these community guidelines, report them to the moderators or administrators by right-clicking messages.

Provide clear and detailed information when reporting violations to assist in taking appropriate action.

7. Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may lead to warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the community.

The severity of the consequences will depend on the nature and seriousness of the violation, as determined by the moderators or administrators.

8. Moderator Authority

Moderators have the final say in all matters related to the community guidelines. Their decisions are binding and must be respected by all members.

Attempts to exploit loopholes in the guidelines will not be tolerated. Members are expected to adhere to the spirit of the guidelines, not just the letter.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these guidelines, please contact us using the contact app.

We welcome your feedback and inquiries regarding the community guidelines, and we will strive to address any concerns you may have promptly.

Last Updated: September 2025
